# Prompt — Hackathon Pitch Deck

## Copy-paste prompt

You are a startup pitch coach and enterprise AI architect.

Create a judge-ready slide deck for **Aircraft Maintenance Decision Copilot**.

## Target length

10–12 main slides plus appendix.

## Required slide flow

1. Title and value proposition.
2. Current operational workflow.
3. Problem statement.
4. Why existing search or chatbot approaches are insufficient.
5. Proposed solution.
6. Golden-path use case.
7. C1–C4 architecture.
8. Agentic AI architecture:
   - LLMs
   - harness/orchestrator
   - tools
   - memory
   - context
   - sub-agents
   - agent loop
9. Data ingestion and data models.
10. Demo and results.
11. Evaluation, security, and observability.
12. Enterprise roadmap and business model.
13. Limitations and future work.
14. Team and closing.

## Partner technology mapping

Map partner technologies only where they create a real architectural benefit:

- AWS: cloud and VM
- OpenAI: models and SDK
- Tinyfish: authorized public extraction
- Neo4j: graph knowledge
- Qdrant or pgvector: semantic retrieval
- PostgreSQL or Supabase: relational system of record
- LangSmith or Langfuse: tracing and evaluation
- Prometheus and Grafana: infrastructure monitoring
- Security partner: guardrails and policy enforcement, subject to verified features

## Required output

For each slide provide:

- Slide title.
- One key message.
- Maximum five bullets.
- Recommended visual.
- Speaker notes.
- Evidence or metric needed.
- What not to claim.

Also provide a one-slide architecture diagram in Mermaid and a 3-minute pitch script.

Do not invent demo metrics. Use `TBD` until measured.
