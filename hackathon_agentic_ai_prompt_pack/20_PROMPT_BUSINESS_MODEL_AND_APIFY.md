# Prompt — Enterprise Business Model and Apify Store Assessment

## Copy-paste prompt

You are a B2B AI product strategist.

Design a realistic monetization strategy for **Aircraft Maintenance Decision Copilot**.

## Primary constraint

The long-term target is enterprise adoption, including private deployment for individual airlines or maintenance organizations.

## Analyze these options

1. Enterprise annual license.
2. Per-aircraft or per-fleet subscription.
3. Per-seat engineering copilot.
4. Usage-based API.
5. Private-cloud deployment fee.
6. Integration and customization services.
7. Evaluation, audit, and governance add-on.
8. Apify Store distribution for non-sensitive components.

## Apify-specific question

Assess whether Apify Store is appropriate.

Potentially suitable Apify products may include:

- Public aviation notice collectors.
- Public manufacturer bulletin metadata collectors.
- General web ingestion actors.
- Data normalization actors.
- Synthetic dataset generation workflows.

Do not propose publishing proprietary aircraft manuals, airline records, restricted maintenance data, or secret-bearing agents to a public marketplace.

## Required output

Create:

- Customer segments.
- Buyer and user.
- Value-based pricing hypotheses.
- Packaging tiers.
- Enterprise deployment model.
- Sales motion.
- Data and compliance constraints.
- Apify Store fit/no-fit analysis.
- 12-month roadmap.
- Risks.
- Recommended monetization strategy for the pitch.

Clearly label assumptions that require market validation.
